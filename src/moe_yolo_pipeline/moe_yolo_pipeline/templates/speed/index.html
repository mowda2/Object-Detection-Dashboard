💡 Calibration Tips

  • Choose 4 points that form a rectangle on the road plane (e.g., lane marking corners)
  • Measure the real-world distances between points in meters
  • Order points consistently clockwise starting from top-left
  • Larger calibration areas give better accuracy for speed measurement
  • Avoid points that are not on the road surface (e.g., curbs, barriers)
